Election to Fellowship is based on training and specialty board certification in cardiology. Members are expected to conform to high moral standards.

What You Need to Apply

  • Receipt of the Eligiblity Survey with a Score of 100+ points. (Launch Eligibility Survey)
  • Two letters of sponsorship from current FACCs. View sponsor letter requirements.
    (One letter may be from the president of the cardiovascular society of the applicant's country, even if they are not an FACC.)
  • Copies of your medical diplomas translated to English
  • An employment verification letter. View letter requirements.
  • A CV or bibliography outlining your publications in a CV-related, peer-reviewed journal, indexed in PubMed or Scopus. View bibliography submission guidelines.
