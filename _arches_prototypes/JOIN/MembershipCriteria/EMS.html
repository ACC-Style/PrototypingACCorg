To apply, candidates must meet one of the following requirements:

  • Be a member of a recognized medical or emergency services professional society, or
  • Upload a sponsorship letter from a current Fellow of the American College of Cardiology with their application.
    • Sponsors should be well acquainted with the applicant’s professional activities. They may not be relatives.
    • The letter(s) should be originally written, on the sponsor’s business letterhead and highlight the applicant’s professional accomplishments, such as hospital, medical society or educational leadership.
