Method of Participation and Claiming Credit

You may claim credit for this Product up to the maximum credit amounts noted above based on the number of hours You spend watching presentations. You may re-enter the Product as many times as You wish, but You can claim credit only once and must claim credit by 11:59 p.m. March 1, 2023.

To receive credit for this Product, You must:

  1. Purchase the Product
  2. Read the Credit Information when You first enter the Product.
  3. View the presentations of your choosing.
  4. Click credits from the left navigation bar after You have completed viewing all presentations for which You intend to claim credit.
  5. Complete the evaluation.
  6. Enter the number of credits You wish to claim. Credits claimed should reflect the total number of hours that You spent watching presentations.
  7. If you are an ABIM or ABP Diplomate, You will need to enter your ABIM or ABP ID and Date of Birth to claim MOC points.
  8. Generate and print your certificate. If You are an ABIM or ABP Diplomate and claimed MOC points, your completion information will be transmitted to the ACCME/ABIM/ABP on your behalf.
