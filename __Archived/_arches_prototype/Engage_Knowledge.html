Engagement Opportunities

ACC provides a myriad of opportunities for engagement with educational content. Learn more about those below.

Just Beginning My Engagement Journey

New to the college thse are a great place to start.

  • Attend How to Become a Cardiovascular Investigator This course provides essential skills to Fellows in Training and Medical Residents on how to get started in career of clinical investigation and research. Get the skills you need to start your research career. Learn more
  • Attend a Teaching Skills Workshop Teaching skills workshops for emerging faculty, FITs and the cardiovascular team provide mentorship and guidance on advancing this skill set. Sign Up
  • Apply for the Emerging Faculty Program Hone your skills as a faculty member, build your network and get noticed as part of this program designed to help identify future faculty leaders of the College. Apply Today
  • Submit an Abstract Submit an abstract for an upcoming course and see your idea in action. Review Submition Guidance
  • Join a Clinical Member Section & Work Group ACC’s clinical member sections influence the development of education and the dissemination of science. Get involved with a Section to help write for one of ACC’s clinical topic collections, plan specialty focused courses related to your area of expertise and work on submissions to ACC’s JACC journals. All Clinical Sections

Able to Give Some Time

  • Assist with Question Writing Help write and edit questions for ACC’s educational programs and products and provide an opportunity for others to learn and fill their knowledge gaps.
  • Provide Peer-to-Peer Support Mentor an up-and-coming faculty member and provide guidance and advice.
  • Answer Clinical Questions in DocMatter When members submit clinical questions to DocMatter for help—you can be there to assist! Serve as a subject matter expert and provide guidance and mentorship on clinical cases.

Able to Give a Lot of Time

  • Apply for SAP Product Content Development As a content developer, you will work closely to outline and develop ACC’s future self-assessment and educational products.
  • Serve as Faculty on a Course As a faculty member, you’ll shape the content of a live course, plan educational opportunities and present the latest learning and science.
  • Work on a Grant-Related Product You can help us on grant related products and building them out.
  • Apply for the ACC.org Editorial Board As an editorial Board member, you’ll help influence the clinical and educational content on ACC.org, our flagship website.
  • Submit a Session Proposal for ACC.25 Help us create a vibrant space for learning and growing at our next Annual Scientific Session by submitting your own proposal for content.
