Become an Affiliate Member

Non-cardiovascular physicians, scientists, or professionals are eligible for this category of membership.

An Affiliate shall have recognized/accredited education, training, certification/licensure, and/or formal appointment at a recognized institution relevant and customary for the professional type.

Caring for the heart is a responsablity of so many professions. Cardiovascular Veterinarians, EMS Professionals and many other professionals are invited to apply for the Affiliate membership.
