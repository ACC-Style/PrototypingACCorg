To apply, candidates must NOT:

Be eligible for ACC membership in any of the existing categories.

Category will be limited to training program coordinators, administrators and managers at general, pediatric, and surgery cardiology training programs; sub-specialty cardiology training programs (e.g., interventional, heart failure, imaging, EP); recognized nursing, physician assistant and clinical pharmacy programs; and recognized cardiology training programs in countries outside the US and Canada.

To apply, candidates must:

  • Administrators for nursing, physician assistant and clinical pharmacy shall be in programs accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education (CCNE); Accreditation Review Commission on Education for the Physician Assistants, Inc. (ARC-PA); or Accreditation Council for Pharmacy Education (ACPE).
  • Applicants must spend at least 75% of their professional time in the role of training program administrator for a CV-related training program.
