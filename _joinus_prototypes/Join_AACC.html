Associate of the American College of Cardiology (AACC)

Reserved for nationally Board-certified cardiovascular professionals, this professional credential is our advanced membership offering, and bestows upon qualified applicants some of the most distinguished credentials offered by the College. It signals to patients and peers that the recipient holds advanced training, education and commitment to quality.

Already a Member?

  • Access your profile
  • Renew your membership
  • Personalize your ACC.org

Qualified Professionals Membership is open to the following professionals:

  • Clinical Nurse Specialists
  • PAs
  • Nurse Practitioners
  • Clinical Pharmacists
  • Registered Nurses
  • Genetic Counselors
  • Cardiovascular Technologists (Sonographers*, Electrophysiology Specialists*, Invasive Specialists*, Vascular Specialists*, Perfusionists)
  • Cardiovascular Rehabilitation Professionals (Clinical Psychologists, Clinical Social Workers, Exercise Physiologists, Occupational Therapists, Physical Therapists, Registered Dietitians)

*Certified by Cardiovascular Credentialing International or the American Registry of Diagnostic Medical Sonography
