**Targets for refactored views:**

| Page type | Word count aim |

| Initiative hub intro | 80–150 words before CTAs |

## ACC gap summary

> ACC stands out for being much more **academic and technical** — not designed for a general or even lightly medical audience.

Improvement path for refactored initiatives:
1. Lower reading level (shorter words, shorter sentences)
2. Define jargon on first use; remove insider assumptions
3. Cut total word count toward ESC/HFSA range
4. Keep scannability and CTA clarity gains from refactor UI

For structural patterns peers use, see [competitive-analysis](../competitive-analysis/SKILL.md).
