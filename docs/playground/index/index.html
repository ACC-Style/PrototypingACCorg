Building a New Board

Cardiology has evolved into a distinct medical specialty, requiring extensive knowledge and training specific to the prevention, diagnosis and treatment of human cardiovascular system disease that is separate from the field of internal medicine. With this evolution, it is time for a new approach to recognizing and sustaining clinical competency in cardiovascular medicine.

Over the past year

Representatives from the major cardiovascular societies have worked collaboratively to put together concepts for a new board of cardiovascular medicine that understands and recognizes the knowledge, skills, and attitudes required to effectively sustain professional excellence and provide cardiovascular patients with the utmost confidence in their care. In the coming weeks, plans are in place to submit an application to the American Board of Medical Specialties (ABMS) requesting the creation of this new board.

Now is the time to change the conversation!
