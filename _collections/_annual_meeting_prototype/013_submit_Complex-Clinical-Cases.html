Submission Status

Complex Clinical Case submissions for ACC.26 are now closed. Accepted case presenters will have a chance to be featured at ACC.26, where they will help shape the future of cardiovascular medicine by sharing their research with the global cardiovascular community. Attendees will have the opportunity to attend case presentations and engage in discussions where groundbreaking research, real-world cases and innovative care converge.

Case Presentation Overview

Case sessions highlight interesting clinical scenarios based on the decision-making used to arrive at a diagnosis or treatment. Each case must illustrate clinical decision-making and include clear teaching points.

Cases are peer-reviewed, and the first author and presenter must be a medical student, resident, fellow in training, medical doctor or researcher.
