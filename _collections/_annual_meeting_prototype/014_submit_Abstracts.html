Submission Status

Abstract submissions for ACC.26 are now closed. Accepted abstract presenters will have a chance to be featured at ACC.26, where they will help shape the future of cardiovascular medicine by sharing their research with the global cardiovascular community. Attendees will have the opportunity to attend abstract presentations and be part of the global stage where groundbreaking research, real-world cases and innovative care converge.

Topic Areas and Featured Fields

Abstracts were submitted across 60 topic areas, and all submissions will be considered for oral, moderated poster and traditional flatboard poster presentations. Featured Fields categories included:

  • Cardio-Obstetrics
  • Cardio-Oncology
  • Critical Care Cardiology
  • CV Team Based Care & Practice Management
  • Geriatric Cardiology
  • Global Cardiovascular Health
  • Innovation, Digital Health and Technology
  • Sports and Exercise
  • Training and Lifelong Learning

Publication and Visibility

Accepted abstracts will be published in an online issue of the Journal of the American College of Cardiology, one of the top-ranked cardiovascular journals in the world. With more than 250 reporters representing 23 countries expected to cover ACC.26, research presented at the meeting can receive significant global visibility. Abstracts and other meeting activities often generate worldwide media interest and billions of media impressions.
