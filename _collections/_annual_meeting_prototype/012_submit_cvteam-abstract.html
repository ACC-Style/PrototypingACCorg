Submission Status

CV Team abstract submissions are now closed. Accepted presenters will have the chance to be featured at ACC.26, where they can help shape the future of cardiovascular medicine by sharing their research with the global cardiovascular community. Attendees will have the opportunity to attend abstract presentations and be part of the global stage where groundbreaking research, real-world cases and innovative care converge.

Submission Categories

CV Team abstracts were accepted in the CVT Research and CVT Pharmacy Resident Research categories. All submissions were considered for oral, moderated poster and traditional flatboard poster presentations.

Eligibility

CV Team abstracts are peer reviewed, and both the presenting author and first author must be CV Team members to submit in this category. If you do not fall under one of the professions below, please submit under the main abstract category.

Eligible Professions

Allied Health Professionals

  • Cardiovascular Technologists (Sonographers, Electrophysiology Specialists, Invasive Specialists, Vascular Specialists, Perfusionists)
  • Cardiovascular Rehabilitation Professionals (Clinical Psychologists, Clinical Social Workers, Exercise Physiologists, Occupational Therapists, Physical Therapists, Registered Dietitians)
  • Genetic Counselors
  • Registered Nurses

APRNs, PAs and Clinical Pharmacists / Advanced Practice Providers (APPs)

  • Clinical Nurse Specialists
  • Clinical Pharmacists
  • Nurse Practitioners
  • PAs
