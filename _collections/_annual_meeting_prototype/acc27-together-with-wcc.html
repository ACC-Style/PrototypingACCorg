ACC.27 Together With WCC

Where the cardiovascular community comes to see what’s next.

For three days, clinicians, researchers, educators and cardiovascular care teams from around the world come together to examine new science, discuss the evidence behind it and learn how it will influence patient care.

Whether you’re presenting research, looking for practical clinical updates or connecting with colleagues across specialties and countries, ACC.27 Together With WCC is where those conversations happen.

The Annual Scientific Session

Each year, ACC brings cardiovascular professionals together to share new science, put evidence in context and strengthen collaboration across the global CV community.

What You ’ ll Find at ACC.27-WCC

  • New cardiovascular science Presented by investigators from around the world.
  • Clinical discussions Putting new evidence into context for practice.
  • Guideline-focused education Designed to help translate research into patient care.
  • Present your own work Share research and receive feedback from leaders in the field.
  • Conversations that continue Beyond the lecture hall with colleagues, collaborators and industry.

Together With the World Heart Federation

ACC.27 is being held together with the World Heart Federation, bringing a broader international perspective to the meeting and creating new opportunities for collaboration across countries, health systems and areas of cardiovascular practice.
