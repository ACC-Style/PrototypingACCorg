The Annual Scientific Session

ACC’s Annual Scientific Session brings together cardiovascular professionals from around the world to share research, discuss emerging evidence and explore the latest advances shaping cardiovascular medicine.

Every year, the meeting serves as a forum for new science, clinical education and professional collaboration across the cardiovascular community.

What You’ll Find at ACC.27-WCC

  • New cardiovascular science presented by investigators from around the world.

  • Clinical discussions that put new evidence into context.

  • Guideline-focused education designed to help translate research into patient care.

  • Opportunities to present your own work and receive feedback from leaders in the field.

  • Conversations that continue beyond the lecture hall with colleagues, collaborators and industry.

Together With the World Heart Federation

ACC.27 is being held together with the World Heart Federation, bringing a broader international perspective to the meeting and creating new opportunities for collaboration across countries, health systems and areas of cardiovascular practice.
