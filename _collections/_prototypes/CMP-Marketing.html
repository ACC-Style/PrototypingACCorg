Performance Assessments

Performance Assessments are a key element of the CMP, with two windows offered each year during the Fall to give participants two opportunities to pass the assessment. During each window, you may take the Performance Assessment ONCE at your convenience any time of day or night. You do not need to schedule a specific time! 

Learn more about Performance Assessments and steps for users already enrolled in the CMP.

Pricing

1. CMP is Free for SAP Subscribers

The CMP is delivered through ACC's Self-Assessment Programs (SAPs) and is free with an existing relevant SAP subscription. SAP subscribers can now choose either a 5-year or 1-year subscription term.

2. Relevant SAP Pricing

  • Member

    $1500/5 year
    purchase plan

    Annual ABIM MOC fee*

  • NonMember

    $1900/5 year
    purchase plan

    Annual ABIM MOC fee*

  • Fellows In Training or Emeritus Members

    $900/5 year
    purchase plan

    Annual ABIM MOC fee*

3. ABIM Fees

In addition, you will need to enroll in the MOC program through ABIM and pay an annual MOC fee directly to ABIM for your chosen MOC option. Learn more about the annual ABIM's MOC fees to participate in CMP by visiting ABIM's website.
