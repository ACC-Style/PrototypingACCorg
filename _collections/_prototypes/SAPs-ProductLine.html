Designed for lifelong learning and tailored to your specialty, SAPs help you stay current, meet certification requirements, and grow in your clinical practice.

What is a SAP?

The ACC's SAPs are a comprehensive learning experience

  • Smart Study Tools that adapt to your goals
  • Expertly-authored educational materials
  • Board-style questions and supportive resources

Why do they work

ACC’s SAPs are trusted by thousands of cardiology professionals because they are carefully structured to align with the ABIM exam blueprint, ensuring that you're not just memorizing facts, but learning how to apply knowledge in real clinical situations.
