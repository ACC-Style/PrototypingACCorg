SAPs are designed for lifelong learning and tailored to your specialty, SAPs help you stay current, meet certification requirements, and grow in your clinical practice.

What’s a SAP?

SAPs are a comprehensive learning experience — combining:

  • Expert-authored educational material
  • Board-style practice questions and exams
  • Smart study tools that adapt to your goals

Why SAPs Work

ACC’s Self-Assessment Programs are trusted by thousands of cardiology professionals because they go far beyond traditional test prep. Each SAP is carefully structured to align with the ABIM exam blueprint, ensuring that you're not just memorizing facts — you're learning how to apply knowledge in real clinical situations.

Choose Your SAP

Find the Self-Assessment Program that fits your specialty:

ACCSAP covers the entire field of adult clinical cardiology.

CathSAP covers the entire field of interventional cardiology.

EP SAP covers the entire field of clinical cardiac electrophysiology.

HF SAP covers the entire field of advanced heart failure and transplant cardiology.

ECHO SAP covers the entire field of clinical cardiac echocardiography.

Coming inside each SAP:

  • Custom quizzes that mimic Board format
  • Highlighting & bookmarking for fast review
  • Moible and Tablet Access
  • Yearly science updates
  • Closed-captioned videos for accessibility
  • Flashcard-friendly printable materials
