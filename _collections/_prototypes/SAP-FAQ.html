FAQ

What Do the Expiration Dates on the Dashboard Mean?

The expiration date is the last date that you can claim credit for the current version of the topic. The day after the expiration date, ACC will publish a new version of the topic that will offer brand new credit. To claim the new credit, you simply have to answer the newly published practice questions for that topic.

What are Term of Approval/Date of CME & MOC Expiration?

The Expiration Dates Calculated are Rolling. Each topic's Term of Approval/Date of CME & MOC Expiration are displayed on the product's dashboard.

Is there a Refund Policy?

Yes, you may receive a full refund by canceling your order within 7 days of purchase*. No refunds or term adjustments will be permitted after the first 7 days of purchase.

*Claiming any type of credit for the product during that time will make you ineligible for a refund.
