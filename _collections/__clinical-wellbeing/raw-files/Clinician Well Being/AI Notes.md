Clinician Well-Being Page Refactor

  Purpose of the Refactor
    Move from a legacy link list to a story-driven suite of pages
    Promote ACC’s dedication to clinician well-being
    Help clinicians quickly find support, resources, and initiatives
    Create a scalable structure for future programs and content
    Relocate the page from /Features/ into Tools / Practice Support / Support Resources

  Core Story
    You are not alone
      Burnout is a major issue in cardiology
      Clinician well-being is not just an individual problem
      The pressures clinicians face can come from patients, colleagues, workload, data burden, and systems of care
      ACC is bringing together resources, education, advocacy, and member-driven initiatives to help

  Elevator Pitch
    Long Version
      ACC’s Clinician Well-Being pages help cardiovascular professionals feel seen, supported, and equipped to address the pressures of modern practice.
      Burnout is not just an individual issue, and it is not solved by self-care alone.
      Through member-driven resources, education, advocacy, quality improvement, and focused initiatives, ACC brings together practical tools and trusted guidance to help clinicians navigate burnout, mistreatment, workload burden, and system-level challenges.
    Short Version
      Clinician well-being is essential to the future of cardiovascular care.
      ACC brings together resources, education, advocacy, and member-driven initiatives to help clinicians address burnout, navigate workplace pressures, and sustain meaningful careers in patient care.
    Hero Message
      You are not alone.
      ACC is committed to helping cardiovascular clinicians address burnout, reduce system pressures, and sustain meaningful careers in patient care.

  Project Goals
    Clarify the story
      Explain why clinician well-being matters
      Show why ACC is investing in this work
      Connect isolated resources into a meaningful narrative
    Improve user findability
      Organize content by clinician need
      Make the experience scannable
      Reduce reliance on plain link lists
    Show ACC commitment
      Connect the work to ACC’s mission
      Elevate member-driven support
      Highlight advocacy, education, quality, and workforce connections
    Preserve useful content
      Audit current page content
      Keep evergreen resources
      Refresh COVID-era materials if still valuable
      Retire obsolete or outdated content
    Support future growth
      Account for upcoming initiatives
      Build a flexible structure for webinars, articles, modules, and resource hubs
      Avoid duct-taped additions later
    Humanize the experience
      Use people-focused imagery
      Make the topic feel personal and supportive
      Reduce reliance on abstract brand graphics

  User Value
    I need to feel like ACC understands the pressure I am under
    I need resources that help me address burnout and workplace stress
    I need to know this is not just my personal failure
    I need practical tools, education, and guidance I can use
    I need to find the right resource without digging through a list of links
    I need to see that ACC is advocating for healthier systems, not just telling me to be more resilient

  ACC Value
    Supports the cardiovascular workforce
    Connects to ACC’s mission to transform cardiovascular care and improve heart health
    Demonstrates member value
    Elevates member-driven work group efforts
    Shows ACC leadership on burnout, mistreatment, workload, and system-level burden
    Supports better clinician health as a path to better patient care

  Content Audit

    Keep
      Valuable evergreen content
      Useful webinars
      Useful articles
      Resources still relevant after COVID-specific language is removed

    Refresh
      COVID-era content with lasting value
      Link labels that need context
      Old descriptions that do not explain user value
      Content that can be reframed around clinician need

    Retire
      Obsolete content
      Grant-required content that no longer needs to remain live
      Outdated graphics or COVID-specific framing
      Links with no clear purpose or value
