### Reignite your purpose. Reconnect with peers. Re-energize your career.

The ACC's Cardio Renew program is designed to support mid-career ACC members through a virtual-based curriculum focused on resilience, reflection and reconnection. Cardio Renew promotes well-being through a coaching curriculum focused on self-leadership, emotional intelligence and personal growth.

> Cardio Renew will help CV professionals reconnect with their purpose and strengthen their non-clinical competencies that make practice more rewarding.
> 
> — Retu Saxena, MD, FACC, Cardio Renew Faculty

### Program Highlights and Format

- Seven-month cohort experience
- Six virtual modules (90 minutes) – Session dates detailed in the application link

- In-person meet & greet at ACC.26 (mandatory)
- Optional 1:1 coaching sessions

This program is not CME certified.

### Who May Apply?

- ACC members in good standing
- Mid-career cardiovascular professionals (including CV Team members and program directors)
- Members without prior ACC leadership cohort experience

- Members without current or prior ACC leadership roles
- Applications must be completed and submitted by **Jan. 30, 2026**. Only completed applications received by the deadline will be considered.

### Why Cardio Renew?

Burnout is a growing concern among cardiovascular professionals, especially women and mid-career clinicians. Cardio Renew will provide a safe, supportive space to explore personal values, understand the role of emotional resilience and reconnect with your Why.

What You'll Gain

- Tools for emotional regulation and self-leadership
- Enhanced communication and decision-making skills
