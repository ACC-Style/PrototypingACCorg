**Podcasts**

- [Practice Made Perfect: Telehealth: Ensuring Quality Cardiovascular Care During Uncertain Times](https://www.acc.org/latest-in-cardiology/articles/2020/03/20/21/25/practice-made-perfect-telehealth)  
	In this episode, **Alison Bailey, MD, FACC**, hosts **Ami Bhatt, MD, FACC**, and **Andrew Freeman, MD, FACC**, in a discussion on the COVID-19 pandemic and how to prioritize patient safety while setting up telemedicine services.
- [Practice Made Perfect: Clinician Well-Being During a Time of Crisis](https://www.acc.org/latest-in-cardiology/articles/2020/03/23/15/00/practice-made-perfect-well-being-during-a-time-of-crisis)  
	In this episode, **Alison Bailey, MD, FACC**, hosts **Laxmi Mehta, MD, FACC**, and **Rosanne Nelson** in a discussion on the COVID-19 pandemic, how the cardiovascular care team is dealing with the resulting stressors, and how to find ways to care for yourself during this time of crisis.
- [MedAxiom HeartTalk Podcast: Staying Calm in the Storm](https://www.medaxiom.com/index.php?src=directory&view=ResourceCenter&srctype=hearttalk_lister)  
	In an episode of the MedAxiom HeartTalk podcast, three experts who have been handling COVID-19 from the front lines share their unique perspectives on how to manage the human impact this pandemic is having on care teams and offer advice on *staying calm in the storm*.

**ACC Member Hub**

- [Member Hub’s Well-Being and Burnout Interest Group](https://memberhub.acc.org/groups/home/43)  
	There’s no one-size-fits-all approach to avoiding burnout – particularly during a crisis like the COVID-19 pandemic. There is value, though, in sharing what works and what doesn’t with colleagues who understand the challenges you face. Join the Well-Being and Burnout group on the [Member Hub, ACC’s online community](https://memberhub.acc.org/dashboard), and get and give strategies for maintaining physical, emotional and mental well-being.
