Why Clinician Well-Being Matters Burnout is a workforce and patient-care issue — not a personal failure.

Burnout is widespread among cardiovascular professionals. Pressures come from patients, colleagues, workload, data burden, and systems of care — not from lacking resilience alone.

Clinician well-being is embedded in ACC’s strategic plan because healthier clinicians are essential to better cardiovascular care.
