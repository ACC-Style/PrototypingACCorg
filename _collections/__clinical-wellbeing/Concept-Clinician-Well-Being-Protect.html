Communicating With Your Team in High-Stress Times

When people are stressed, they struggle to hear and remember information. Lead with empathy. Limit key messages to three to five. Repeat what matters most at the beginning and end.

When a Colleague Is Struggling

Most cardiologists who need help will not ask for it. Post the ACC well-being infographic in your break room. Know your institution’s EAP. Normalize the conversation. If a colleague is targeted by uncivil behavior, see how to respond as a witness or supporter.
