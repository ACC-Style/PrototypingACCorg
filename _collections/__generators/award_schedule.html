Generic date-oriented list pattern. Items render in a flat CMS-style data-role="items" list, then client-side JavaScript toggles each card into Open, Upcoming, or Past. This generator uses awards as the example dataset.

Expected columns

  1. title
  2. start_date (MM-DD, evergreen — current year applied at runtime)
  3. end_date (MM-DD, optional)
  4. city
  5. country
  6. flags — comma-separated (e.g. FIT, International)
  7. description
  8. learn_more_url
  9. action_url — optional CTA when item is open (e.g. apply link)

Rows sort chronologically by start_date, then alphabetically by title (leading The and ACC are ignored). Section headings and labels are configurable on the root data-component="DateList" element.
