Why Should You Join {{page.section_name}}?

The Women in Cardiology Section of the ACC offers a platform and opportunities to strengthen their professional support system, skills and the future of the community. Active involvement in the Section provides leadership skills developemnt and understanding the governance structure and avenues to leadership within the College.

Mission

Our mission is to foster a supportive community that advocates for gender and career equity, empowering women cardiologists across all specialties and career stages. Join us in creating a more inclusive and dynamic field in cardiovascular medicine.
