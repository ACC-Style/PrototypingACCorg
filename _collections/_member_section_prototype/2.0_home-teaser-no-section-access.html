Why Should You Join {{page.section_name}}?

The Women in Cardiology Section of the ACC is a powerful platform for women physicians to build connections, grow as leaders and strengthen their careers and professional support system. Members advocate for gender and career equity, engage in mentorship and networking, and promote wellness for women within the cardiology community.

Mission

Our Mission is to advance the careers of women in cardiology by fostering mentorship, professional development and leadership opportunities; promoting gender equity in training programs and the workforce; and inspiring the next generation to pursue careers in cardiovascular medicine.
