WIC Virtual Book Club

The ACC Women in Cardiology (WIC) Book Club connects members through virtual discussions exploring personal growth and leadership beyond clinical medicine. Open to all WIC Member Section participants.

  • Connect with Peers Nationwide
    Join a vibrant conversation with Women in Cardiology members and strengthen the WIC community together.
  • Explore Self & Career Growth Topics
    Discuss books, articles, and podcasts focused on leadership, resilience, and personal development.
  • Join the Conversation on Twitter
    Participate live using #WICBOOK and #ACCWIC to share insights and questions during each session.
  • Learn from Authors & Experts
    Hear directly from featured authors and thought leaders exploring the intersection of medicine and storytelling.

How It Works

The WIC Communications Work Group selects a book, article, or podcast for a live virtual discussion.

  • Follow and use hashtags #WICBOOK and #ACCWIC to join the conversation.
  • Share your favorite quotes, questions, and insights during the event window.
  • Encourage fellow WIC members to join in and stay active in the community.

Get Involved

Have questions, want to suggest a book, or write a review? Contact Elsie Ansong.

Help grow the WIC Book Club by spreading the word and inviting other members to participate.

About Dr. Suzanne Koven

Dr. Suzanne Koven has practiced primary care internal medicine at Massachusetts General Hospital for over 30 years and holds the Valerie Winchester Family Endowed Chair in Primary Care Medicine. She is the inaugural Writer-in-Residence at Mass General and an associate professor at Harvard Medical School. Her essays and articles have appeared in publications including The New England Journal of Medicine, The Boston Globe, and The New Yorker.
