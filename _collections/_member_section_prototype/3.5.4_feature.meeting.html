WIC Leadership Council: Leadership & Well-Being Summit

Join WIC leaders and allies for a fast-paced half-day focused on leading without losing yourself. Learn practical strategies for psychological safety, assertive communication, and sustainable leadership. Network with peers, hear from nationally recognized faculty, and leave with actionable commitments to bring back to your team.

When and Where

  • Date: Sunday, Oct 5, 2025
  • Time: 8:45 a.m. - 2:25 p.m. (ET)
  • Location: Hyatt Regency Washington on Capitol Hill Washington, DC
  • Meeting Room: Capitol AB
