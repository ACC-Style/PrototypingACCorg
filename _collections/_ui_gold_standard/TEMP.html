Resources

The American College of Cardiology offers a growing library of evidence-based educational resources to support cardiovascular professionals in clinical practice, learning, and teaching. These trusted tools are curated to help translate science into care and support continuous professional development.

  • Accelerating Innovation for Medical Excellence in HCM

    The ACC's Accelerating Innovation for Medical Excellence in Hypertrophic Cardiomyopathy (AIME HCM) initiative is designed to increase awareness and understanding of HCM, activate clinicians, and optimize disease management to improve outcomes.

  • Competencies

    ACC has developed competencies for cardiologists, from training through practice, as well as competencies for nurse practitioners and physician assistants who are members of the cardiovascular care team, defining the knowledge, skills, and behaviors that health care providers, patients, the public and applicable accrediting bodies can reasonably expect these clinicians to achieve, maintain or enhance.

  • Faculty Resource Center

    ACC has developed a resource designed to support cardiology professionals who deliver training and education in a variety of settings. If you teach medical students, present at conferences, or develop curricula, this page is for you.

  • Fellowship Program Director Tools and Resources

    Featuring content developed for Fellowship Program Directors, this section includes curriculum development resources, innovative learning tools, assessment tools, training and accreditation requirements, and tools for faculty development. Requires ACC.org login.
