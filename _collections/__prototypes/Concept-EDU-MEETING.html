Includes Access to CMP

Collaborative Maintenance Pathway (CMP) is an alternative option for physicians to maintain Board certification that focuses on 20% of the field each year so you can remain 100% certified. Every 5 years, your knowledge is refreshed on the entire field of cardiology—learning never stops.
