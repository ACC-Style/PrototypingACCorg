<h3 style="color:red">Zone 6: How We Publish</h3>

<span style="color:red">**UI Component:** Main content + sidebar layout. { Anchor ID: #How-We-Publish }</span>

<span style="color:red">**Section Headline (H2)**</span>  
How We Publish

<span style="color:red">**Lead Paragraph**</span>  
Every manuscript undergoes a transparent, expert-driven process that upholds the highest standards of scientific integrity while respecting authors' time.

<span style="color:red">**Subsection: The Editorial Process**</span>  
<span style="color:red">{ Ordered list with bold step name + dash + description }</span>

1. **Submission** — Authors submit via the JACC online system; each journal accepts research, reviews, editorials, and specialty content.
2. **Editorial Triage** — Editors evaluate for merit, novelty, and scope; manuscripts are assigned to handling editors.
3. **Peer Review** — Two or more external experts evaluate methodology, results, and conclusions under strict confidentiality.
4. **Decision & Revision** — Most accepted papers go through at least one revision to strengthen the final work.
5. **Publication** — Accepted manuscripts are copyedited, typeset, and published online with DOIs and PubMed indexing.
6. **Dissemination** — High-impact work is highlighted through ACC channels, media, and email alerts.
