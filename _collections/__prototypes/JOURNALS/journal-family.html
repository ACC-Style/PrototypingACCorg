The JACC Journals

The JACC Journals portfolio brings together leading cardiovascular research, clinical insight, and specialty-focused education across the field.

  • JACC

    Top research across all cardiovascular medicine—landmark trials, guidelines, and state-of-the-art reviews.

    Best for: All cardiovascular professionals

  • JACC: Cardiovascular Interventions

    Coronary, structural, peripheral and interventional medicine.

    Best for: Interventional cardiologists, structural heart teams

  • JACC: Cardiovascular Imaging

    Clinical applications of noninvasive and invasive imaging across echo, CT, MRI, and nuclear.

    Best for: Imaging specialists, multimodality readers

  • JACC: Heart Failure

    Clinical studies, experimental investigations, and emerging therapies in heart failure.

    Best for: HF specialists, transplant & advanced therapies

  • JACC: Clinical Electrophysiology

    Epidemiology, pathogenesis, diagnosis, and treatment of cardiac arrhythmias.

    Best for: Electrophysiologists, device specialists

  • JACC: Basic to Translational Science

    Novel discoveries bridging bench science to clinical outcomes.

    Best for: Basic & translational researchers

  • JACC: Case Reports

    Clinical cases, vignettes, and how-we-do-it, and more, focused on problem solving.

    Best for: Fellows, clinicians with novel cases

  • JACC: CardioOncology

    Multidisciplinary CV care for cancer patients and survivors.

    Best for: Cardio-oncology teams

  • JACC: Asia

    Original research focused on Asian populations and region-specific CV challenges.

    Best for: Clinicians serving Asian populations

  • JACC: Advances

    General cardiology and all subspecialties, welcoming authors at every career stage.

    Best for: Early-career investigators, broad CV topics
