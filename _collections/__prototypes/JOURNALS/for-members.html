For Members & Authors

Whether you read, write, review, or lead—the JACC Journals connect directly to your practice and career.

Member Benefits

  • Full Journal Access ACC Members receive complimentary online access to all JACC journals and archives.
  • Alerts & Digests Customizable email alerts by topic, journal, or article type.
  • CME & MOC Credit Earn credit through JACC journal-based educational activities.
  • Author Opportunities Submit research, serve as a reviewer, or contribute as an editorial leader.

Paths to Contribute

  • Submit Research — JACC’s breadth means there’s a home for nearly every type of cardiovascular manuscript.
  • Peer Review — Build expertise and serve the profession through scholarly review.
  • Editorial Leadership — ACC members serve as editors and board members, shaping cardiovascular science publishing.
  • Early-Career Pathways — JACC: Advances and JACC: Case Reports are designed specifically for emerging investigators.

Journals + Education

  • Guidelines published in JACC inform clinical tools
  • Meeting presentations become JACC articles
