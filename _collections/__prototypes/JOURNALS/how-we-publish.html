How We Publish

Every manuscript undergoes a transparent, expert-driven process that upholds the highest standards of scientific integrity while respecting authors’ time.

The Editorial Process

  1. Submission — Authors submit via the JACC online system; each journal accepts a wide range of content for any research or cases you want to publish.
  2. Editorial Triage — Editors evaluate for merit, novelty, and scope; manuscripts are assigned to handling editors.
  3. Peer Review — Two or more external experts evaluate methodology, results, and conclusions under strict confidentiality.
  4. Decision & Revision — Most accepted papers go through at least one revision to strengthen the final work.
  5. Publication — Accepted manuscripts are copyedited, typeset, and published online with DOIs and PubMed indexing.
  6. Dissemination — High-impact work is highlighted through ACC and JACC channels, media, and email alerts.

Our Principles

  • Editorial Independence Publication decisions based solely on scientific merit.
  • Ethics & Integrity ICMJE guidelines, conflict-of-interest disclosure, anti-plagiarism policies.
  • Open Access Five titles are fully OA; all journals offer author-choice open access.
  • Elsevier Partnership ACC’s clinical leadership combined with world-class production and distribution.

Speed & Efficiency

  • ~30 days average first decision
  • Online publication of acceptance
  • Weekly publication for JACC, all journals publish content as soon as it is available
  • Rapid pathway for expedited late-breaking clinical trials presented at meetings

Standards

  • ICMJE — Authorship & disclosure
  • COPE — Publication ethics
  • CONSORT/PRISMA — Reporting standards
  • CRediT — Contributor roles
  • FAIR — Data sharing
