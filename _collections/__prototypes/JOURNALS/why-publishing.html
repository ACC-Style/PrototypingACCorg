Why We Publish

Journals are where cardiovascular science becomes actionable. Peer-reviewed publishing creates the trusted evidence base that underpins guidelines, clinical decisions, and patient outcomes.

What Publishing Provides

  • Peer Review Expert evaluation ensures sound, ethical research reaches clinicians.
  • Trusted Evidence The foundation for guidelines, clinical decisions, and quality improvement.
  • Global Discoverability Indexed in PubMed, Web of Science, and accessible in 175+ countries, including five open access journals freely available to readers anywhere & everywhere.
  • Translation to Practice Expert commentaries, reviews, and guidelines bridge science to bedside care.
