Why Cardiology?

Cardiovascular disease is the world’s leading cause of death. In cardiology, you can change outcomes for individual patients and improve public health at scale.

  • Make a measurable impact

    Treat conditions from congenital heart disease to heart failure — where innovation saves lives.

  • Choose from many career shapes

    Clinical practice, research, industry, education, and advocacy — cardiology rewards diverse talents.

  • Join a diverse profession

    Physicians, nurses, pharmacists, PAs, and technologists all play essential roles on the CV Team.
