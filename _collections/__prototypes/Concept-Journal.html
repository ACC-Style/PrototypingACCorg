Publishing That Advances Cardiovascular Care How the ACC’s journal program connects science to practice.

The ACC publishes not merely to disseminate information, but to transform discovery into trusted evidence that clinicians use every day. Through its partnership with JACC Journals, the College ensures that guidance is rigorously developed, widely disseminated, and accessible at the point of care.
